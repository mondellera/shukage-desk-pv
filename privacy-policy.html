Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 (Bundled)
Open latest online version →

ShuKage does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. All information you create — videos, markers, recordings, tags, notes, settings, and practice history — is stored on your own device.

What ShuKage is

ShuKage is a Chrome extension for shadowing practice on YouTube. It lets you create A-B markers, loop selected video segments, record your voice, and review your practice history directly in your browser.

What data is stored

ShuKage stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's built-in browser storage mechanisms:

  • YouTube video URLs and titles you register in the library
  • Markers, A-B positions, ratings, tags, and notes you create
  • Audio recordings you make using the microphone
  • Practice statistics, recording history, and rating history
  • Extension settings and preferences

This data is stored using chrome.storage.local and IndexedDB. It does not leave your device unless you explicitly export it using the built-in export feature.

Microphone access

ShuKage requests microphone access only when you initiate a recording. Audio data is processed locally in your browser and saved to your device's local browser storage. No audio is transmitted to any server.

YouTube access

ShuKage operates on supported YouTube pages to provide playback controls, marker management, recording features, and related language-learning tools. This includes reading publicly available video metadata (such as the video's audio language and caption tracks) to automatically fill in marker details like the language field. It does not read, collect, or transmit your YouTube account information, private account data, or YouTube viewing history.

No external servers

The ShuKage extension itself does not send data to any external server, analytics service, advertising service, or third party. There are no accounts, no sign-ins, no cloud sync, and no advertising features.

No analytics or tracking

ShuKage does not use analytics tools, tracking pixels, advertising identifiers, or third-party tracking scripts. The extension does not monitor your activity outside the supported pages where its features are used.

Data export and deletion

You can export your library data at any time using the export feature. Exported backup files are created only when you choose to export them, and they remain wherever you save them.

To delete ShuKage data, use the cleanup tools within the extension where available, or remove the extension from Chrome. Removing the extension normally deletes extension data stored by Chrome, but exported backup files remain outside the extension and must be deleted separately if you no longer want them.

Data sharing

ShuKage does not share your data with anyone. Because the extension does not collect or transmit your data, there is no server-side database, user account, or cloud profile associated with your use of ShuKage.

Children's privacy

ShuKage is intended as a personal language-learning tool. It does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the new version will be made available on this page with an updated date. Significant changes will be noted in the extension's release notes or support documentation.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how ShuKage handles data, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.

Please do not include personal information, private recordings, exported backup files, authentication tokens, or other sensitive data in public GitHub issues.
