What ShuKage is

ShuKage is a Chrome extension designed to support shadowing practice, listening practice, recording review, and personal note-taking on YouTube and other supported video pages.

ShuKage provides tools such as A-B markers, loop playback, voice recording, tags, notes, ratings, and practice history directly in your browser.
